The Foundation

I created Big Sky Strong because I felt inspired to share my experience with fitness and health. My hope is that people will read my stories and my tips to learn about their own health. I want people to use my website to find fun recipies and to share tips and tricks to hack fitness and health. This does not mean trying to take some magic pill and hoping your life will change. I promote fitness and nutrition that is really hard to acquire. It is painfull and tough, but to those who have the motivation, your results will be positive.
