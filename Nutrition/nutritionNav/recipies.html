Egg White Heaven
  • 4oz Egg Whites
  • 1 slice bread
  • 1oz butter/butter spread
  • 1/2 oz light mayo
  • 1oz Ketchup
  1. Place medium saucepan on the burner set to medium temperature
  2. Spread butter onto both sides of bread and place on saucepan until both sides begin to crisp
  3. Remove bread from saucepan and put egg whites into saucepan and cook untill egg white is done (kind of like an egg white omlette).
  4. Spread light mayo onto the bread
  5. Place the egg whites onto the bread
  6. Drizzle with the ketchup
  7. Enjoy!
